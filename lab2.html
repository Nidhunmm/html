India vs West Indies 2nd ODI Highlights: Axar Patel heroics help IND secure thrilling win, seal series against WI

IND vs WI, 2nd ODI Highlights: Axar Patel's unbeaten 64 off 35 balls helped India claim a thrilling two-wicket win and gain an unassailable 2-0 lead in the three-game series. Sanju Samson also scored 54 off 51 deliveries, adding a 99-run partnership with Shreyas Iyer, who hit 63. Deepak Hooda further chipped in with a 33-run cameo towards the end. With six needed off the last three balls, Axar clobbered Kyle Mayers straight for a six as India scripted a sensational series-clinching win. He smashed five sixes and three fours in his unconquered knock. Earlier, West Indies posted 311 for six batting first, thanks to Shai Hope's 115 against India in the second ODI.
